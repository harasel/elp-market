Our story

Fourteen years of considered brokerage.

ELP Markets was founded on a simple premise: traders deserve a broker whose incentives are aligned with their own. In an industry too often defined by aggressive marketing and shifting promotions, we chose a quieter path — investing in infrastructure, in people, and in the disciplined stewardship of client relationships.

Today we serve a global clientele from offices across three continents, executing across foreign exchange, metals, indices and commodities with the same care we brought to our first account in 2010.

Principles

The commitments that guide us.

Discretion

Client relationships are confidential by default. We do not disclose activity, holdings or identities to third parties.

Transparency

Every fee, spread and swap is published openly. If we cannot explain a cost simply, we do not charge it.

Stability

We are privately held and profitably operated, with no external funding pressure to compromise service.

Craft

Execution is a craft. Our dealing desks are staffed by career professionals, not rotating juniors.

Leadership

Guided by seasoned professionals.

Our leadership brings together decades of experience across institutional trading, treasury management, compliance and technology — chosen for judgement, not headlines.

FAQ

Common questions.

Which jurisdictions do you operate in?

ELP Markets operates under recognised regulatory frameworks and holds licences appropriate to the regions we serve. A full disclosure is available on request.

How are client funds protected?

Client funds are held in segregated accounts with tier-one banks and are never used for corporate operations.

What is the minimum account size?

Standard accounts begin at USD 500. Professional and institutional tiers are available on application.

Do you offer a demo account?

Yes — unlimited-duration demo accounts are available on both MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5.

How do I speak to a relationship manager?

New and existing clients can request an introduction via our contact form; a manager will be in touch within one business day.

Speak with a relationship manager for a candid conversation about fit.
